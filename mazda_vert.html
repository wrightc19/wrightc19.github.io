Mazda RX7 Vert

Modified Second Generation Mazda RX7

One of the very first vehicles I modified is this Mazda RX7. The only thing that is probably stock at this point is the windshield...
Here is a list of some of the modifications made to this car:

  • Exterior
    • Blue Plasti-Dip coating
    • 20 inch Motegi Racing Wheels
    • Removed the roof and turned it into a convertible (thus the vert)
  • Everything Else
    • LQ9 Engine and Transmission taken from a donor Cadillac Escalade
    • Built top and bottom end
    • Straight pipe with cat dumps
    • Naturally Aspirated build
